## Your next step: record and submit your exam videos

You have finished all eight modules — congratulations! Now it's time to **prove what you've learned and earn your certificate**.

The final exam is **five short hands-on videos (5 minutes each)** where you record yourself performing real ServiceNow admin tasks in your own Personal Developer Instance (PDI). You upload them to Google Drive and submit the links through a simple form. Your instructor personally reviews each video against a rubric, sends written feedback, and — once you pass all five — issues your **branded Certificate of Completion**.

{% hint style="success" %}
**How to submit your recorded videos:**

1. Record each of the five exams (screen + voice narration, 5 minutes max each).
2. Upload them to Google Drive and set sharing to *Anyone with the link can view*.
3. Submit the links through the course form — include the email tied to your membership.
4. Get your scored feedback and certificate within 5–7 days.
{% endhint %}

{% hint style="warning" %}
**Video review is for Exam Navigator members (and higher tiers).** Watching the course is free, but your recorded videos are only reviewed, scored, and certified if you're an active [Exam Navigator member](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCMf7pje1x5iZkkEF-Y3PLSA/join) or above.
{% endhint %}
