# Submission Guide — How to Record & Submit Your Exam Videos

**Quick answer:** Record each exam as a screen-capture video (5 minutes max) with your voice narrating, upload it to Google Drive set to *Anyone with the link can view*, then paste the links into the submission form along with the email tied to your Exam Navigator membership. Your instructor reviews within 5–7 days.

{% hint style="warning" %}
**Reviews are for Exam Navigator members (and higher tiers) only.** Make sure your [membership](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCMf7pje1x5iZkkEF-Y3PLSA/join) is active and submit using the matching email/handle, or your videos will not be graded.
{% endhint %}

## Step 1 — Record your screen with narration

You need a screen recorder that captures your screen **and** your microphone. Free options:

| Tool | Platform | Notes |
|------|----------|-------|
| **Loom** (free plan) | Browser/Desktop | Easiest — records screen + voice, gives a shareable link instantly |
| **OBS Studio** (free) | Windows/Mac/Linux | Most powerful, saves a local file |
| **Xbox Game Bar** (Win+G) | Windows | Built in, no install |
| **QuickTime** | Mac | Built in (File → New Screen Recording) |

**Recording tips:**

* Keep each video to **5 minutes maximum**. Plan your steps before you hit record.
* **Narrate out loud** — say what you're doing and *why*. The rubric rewards your explanation, not just the clicks.
* Record in your own **free Personal Developer Instance (PDI)**.
* You don't need to show your face — screen + voice is enough.

## Step 2 — Upload to Google Drive

1. Go to [drive.google.com](https://drive.google.com) and create a folder named, for example, **"ServiceNow Exam — [Your Name]"**.
2. Upload your video file(s) into that folder.
3. For each video (or the folder): right-click → **Share** → under *General access* choose **"Anyone with the link"** → role **Viewer**.
4. Click **Copy link**. Test the link in a private/incognito window to confirm it opens without sign-in.

{% hint style="info" %}
**Why "Anyone with the link"?** If the link is restricted, your instructor cannot open it and your submission cannot be reviewed. Double-check this before submitting.
{% endhint %}

## Step 3 — Submit all five videos in ONE submission

{% hint style="warning" %}
**Submit once, with all five exam videos together.** This is a single submission — record all five exams first, then enter all five Google Drive links in the form and submit one time. Don't send them one at a time.
{% endhint %}

Submit your links through the official course submission form:

{% hint style="success" %}
**Submission form:** [Submit your exam videos here](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSfet-aZcHkVI8LdZ7n7PlNj0DFRxRYvnT59sWmRQ7CbUFKI1Q/viewform)
{% endhint %}

The form will ask for:

* Your **full name** (as you want it to appear on your certificate)
* Your **YouTube channel name / handle used for your membership** — this is how your instructor verifies your membership payment, so it must match the account you joined with
* Your **membership email** (Google account tied to your membership, for cross-checking)
* Your **membership tier**
* A **Google Drive link for all 5 exam videos** (all required in the same submission)
* Whether this is a **resubmission**, plus any notes (optional)

{% hint style="info" %}
**Why we ask for your YouTube channel name:** Your videos are only reviewed for active members. Bill matches the channel name/handle you enter here against the membership (payment) records on the [TechTalk with Bill channel](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCMf7pje1x5iZkkEF-Y3PLSA/join). If they don't match, your submission can't be graded.
{% endhint %}

Submit all five videos together in a single form submission. If you later need to redo one or more exams after feedback, submit a **new** form with all five links again and mark it as a **resubmission** in the form. **You may resubmit as many times as you need** — the goal is mastery.

## Step 4 — Get your review & certificate

* Your instructor reviews each video against its rubric and returns a **score out of 20** plus **written feedback**, within **5–7 days**.
* Pass all five (70+ total) and you receive your **branded Certificate of Completion**.
* Score 50–69 on any exam? You'll get specific feedback and can **re-record and resubmit** that exam.

## Submission checklist

* [ ] I am an active **Exam Navigator** (or higher) member
* [ ] I have recorded **all five** exam videos and am submitting them together
* [ ] Each video is **5 minutes or less** with clear voice narration
* [ ] Videos are uploaded to Google Drive and set to **"Anyone with the link can view"**
* [ ] I tested each link in an incognito window
* [ ] I submitted via the form with my **membership email/handle** and **full name**
