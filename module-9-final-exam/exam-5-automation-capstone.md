# Exam 5 — Automation & Capstone App

**Quick answer:** Record a single video of **5 minutes maximum** demonstrating the tasks below in your own ServiceNow Personal Developer Instance (PDI), narrating as you go. This exam covers **Automation & Workflows + Capstone (Modules 6 & 8)** and is worth **20 points**.

{% hint style="warning" %}
**Members-only review.** Your video is only graded if you are an active **Exam Navigator** member (or any higher tier) of [TechTalk with Bill](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCMf7pje1x5iZkkEF-Y3PLSA/join). Include your membership email/handle when you submit.
{% endhint %}

## The scenario

This is the capstone proof. You must automate a process and walk through the small application you built, showing it all works together.

## What you must demonstrate in your video

Complete and narrate each of the following:

1. Use **Flow Designer** to build a flow (for example, an **approval** or notification flow) triggered by a record change. Show it running.
2. Explain **when you would use a Business Rule vs a Flow vs a UI Policy** — give one example of each.
3. Walk through your **capstone application** from Module 8: show its table(s), a form, and at least one piece of automation working end to end.
4. Show **one report or dashboard** built on your app's data.
5. Reflect: what would you build or improve next, and how does this prove you're ready for the CSA exam?

{% hint style="info" %}
**Time limit: 5 minutes.** Plan your steps before you hit record. Part of the skill is doing this efficiently — just like a real admin under deadline.
{% endhint %}

## Grading rubric (20 points)

| Criterion | What earns full marks | Points |
|-----------|----------------------|:------:|
| **Flow Designer** | Builds and runs a working flow with an appropriate trigger. | 5 |
| **Automation decision-making** | Correctly distinguishes Business Rule vs Flow vs UI Policy with examples. | 4 |
| **Capstone app demo** | Demonstrates a functional app: table, form, and automation working together. | 5 |
| **Reporting** | Shows a relevant report or dashboard on the app's data. | 3 |
| **Reflection & readiness** | Thoughtful reflection on next steps and CSA readiness. | 3 |
| **Total** | | **20** |

Your instructor will score each criterion and return written feedback. **You may resubmit** this exam if you score below the passing line — the goal is mastery and confidence.

## How to submit

1. Record your screen with voice narration (see the [Submission Guide](submission-guide.md)).
2. Upload the video to **Google Drive** and set sharing to *Anyone with the link can view*.
3. Submit the link through the [course submission form](submission-guide.md) — include the **email/handle tied to your Exam Navigator (or higher) membership**.
